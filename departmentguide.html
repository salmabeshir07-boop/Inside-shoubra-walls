Department Guide

the prep-year comes the choice of a lifetime... where will your passion lead you?

  • ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING

    The oldest and largest major, focusing on the generation, transmission, and distribution of electrical energy, as well as machine design...
    • Study Content:
      1. Electrical Machines
      2. Power Systems
      3. High Voltage Engineering
      4. Power Electronics
      5. Protection & Control
      6. Renewable Energy
    • Career Opportunities:

      • Work areas include power generation plants, distribution companies, construction (as an MEP engineer designing building networks), and industrial automation.

    • Who will enjoy it..?

      • Someone who enjoys tackling complex, abstract puzzles. Anyone fascinated by the invisible systems that run our world, from configuring computer networks and routers to understanding how heavy machinery operates.

  • MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

    We understand how everything moves and works

    • Study content:
      1. focuses on energy
      2. machine design
      3. manufacturing
      4. thermos dynamics
      5. fluid mechnaics
    • Sub-departments:

      Power department , production department ,Mechatronics department

    • Career opportunities:

      • HVAC engineer , Power plant , Quality control , Tooling designer , Robotics engineer

    • Who will enjoy it..?

      • people who love machines and logical solving.

  • ARCHITECTURE ENGINEERING

    Where creativity meets reality.

    • Study content:
      1. Blend of art and science
      2. Architectural design
      3. History of art
      4. Building construction
      5. urban planning
    • Career opportunities:

      • Interior design , urban planning , Technecaloffice and BIM , Constructionsite management.

    • Who will enjoy it..?

      • people who love drawing and have a creative vision.

  • GEOMTICS ENGINEERING

    The science of collecting, analyzing, and storing "Spatial Data." Instead of traditional measuring tools, it uses satellites, drones, radar, and advanced GPS to create 3D smart maps and plan cities
    • Study Content:
      1. Geographic Information Systems (GIS)
      2. Remote Sensing
      3. Photogrammetry & LiDAR
      4. Geodesy & GNSS/GPS
      5. Programming Languages
    • Career Opportunities:

      • GIS Analyst/Developer,Surveying/Geomatics Engineer, Remote Sensing Specialist

    • Who will enjoy it..?

      • A person who enjoys looking at the macro level. Instead of designing a single component or circuit, you are analyzing entire landscapes, tracking environmental changes, and planning the infrastructure of new cities

  • CIVIL ENGINEERING

    We build the world and shape the future infrastructure

    • Study content:
      1. Structural construction management
      2. Concrete and steel design
      3. soil mechanics
      4. surveying and hydraulics
    • Career opportunities:

      • Site engineer , Technical office , infrastructure engineer , Quantity surveyor , Geotechnical engineer

    • Who will enjoy it..?

      • people who love seeing their work stand tall and understanding how cities are built

  • COMMUNICATION AND COMPUTER ENGINEERING

    Connect the world and build the digital future

    • Study Content:
      1. Data transmission
      2. signal processing
      3. electronic circuits
      4. microprocessor design.
    • Sub-departments

      • Communications (Wireless & Satellites),Computer (AI & Software architecture),Electronics (Semiconductors)

    • Career Opportunities:

      • Network Engineer (Data infrastructure) Embedded Systems Engineer (Smart hardware),Telecom Engineer (Mobile networks), Software Engineer (App development),Cybersecurity Analyst (Digital protection)

    • Who will enjoy it..?

      • People who love logic, coding, and the art of moving data.
